About Etermon

In 3033, humanity has a tremendous development. Human begins looking for a new civilized outside the earth.
With that growth, we discover many other societies. Many different kinds live on other various planets. On a trip around the universe, one astronaut's spaceship got broken. He has no choice except to land on a strange asteroid.
On this planet, he finds out new species. They are monsters that have the power of different elements. They are Etermon.
To go back to the Earth, the astronaut needs to use the space gate. It is ruled by the master of this planet. The explorer has no alternative. He has to catch and train the Etermon to fight against the master. Then, he can have a chance to go home.

PLAYGAME

The diverse monster hierarchies, matching systems, and arraying based on the characteristics of each monster will enhance and bring the strategy of the game to the forefront.

PLAY2EARN

Most games in the Play-to-Earn system will have unstable prices when game items are inflated. Unlike them, we have Burn mechanism to keep it stable and valuable.
